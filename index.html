Peanut Butter and Jelly

Let's Learn how to make a peanut butter and Jam Sandwhich.

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • Jam
  • Peanut Butter

Instructions

  1. Lay Out Two Peices Of Bread.
  2. Spread Peanut Butter On One Slice.
  3. Spread Jam On The Other Slice.
  4. Stick The Two Slices Together And Enjoy!
